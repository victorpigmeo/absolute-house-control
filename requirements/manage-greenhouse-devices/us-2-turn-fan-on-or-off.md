# US2: Turn the fan on or off

**As a** user
**I want** to turn the greenhouse fan on or off
**So that** I can control ventilation without being physically present

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Turning the fan on
- **Given** the fan is off
- **When** I turn the fan on
- **Then** the fan turns on and the UI shows it as on

### Scenario 2: Turning the fan off
- **Given** the fan is on
- **When** I turn the fan off
- **Then** the fan turns off and the UI shows it as off
