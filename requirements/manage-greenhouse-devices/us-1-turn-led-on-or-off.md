# US1: Turn the LED light on or off

**As a** user
**I want** to turn the greenhouse LED light on or off
**So that** I can control grow lighting without being physically present

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Turning the LED on
- **Given** the LED light is off
- **When** I turn the LED on
- **Then** the LED turns on and the UI shows it as on

### Scenario 2: Turning the LED off
- **Given** the LED light is on
- **When** I turn the LED off
- **Then** the LED turns off and the UI shows it as off
